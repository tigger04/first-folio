#+TITLE: Cold Open
#+AUTHOR: A. Screenwriter
#+SUBTITLE: A Short Film
#+DATE: April 2026
#+VERSION: First Draft

* Characters
|--------+----------------------------------------------|
| Sarah  | Detective. 30s. Sharp, tired, undercaffeinated. |
| Mike   | Her partner. 40s. Talks too much.              |
| Barista | Early 20s. Unimpressed by everything.         |
|--------+----------------------------------------------|

* Act I
** Scene 1
*** INT. COFFEE SHOP — MORNING
*** A queue. Fluorescent light. The hiss of a milk steamer. Sarah stands at the counter, badge visible on her belt.
**** Barista
What can I get you.
**** Sarah
Black coffee. Large.
**** Barista
We don't do large. We do tall, grande, and —
**** Sarah
The biggest one you have.
*** The Barista pours without enthusiasm.
*** Mike enters, out of breath, phone in hand.
**** Mike
We got a call.
**** Sarah
I just ordered.
**** Mike
It's a bad one.
**** Sarah
They're all bad ones.
**** Mike
This one's worse.
*** Sarah looks at the coffee being poured. Looks at Mike. Back at the coffee.
**** Sarah
How much worse.
**** Mike
Remember the Kowalski thing?
**** Sarah
Which Kowalski thing.
**** Mike
Exactly.
*** Beat.
**** Sarah to Barista
Make it a double.
*** Lights down                                                     :transition:

** Scene 2
*** EXT. ALLEYWAY — CONTINUOUS
*** Rain. Blue and red lights strobing off wet brick. Yellow tape. A uniform at the perimeter.
**** Mike
So the call came in at 0600.
**** Sarah
Who found it.
**** Mike
Jogger.
**** Sarah
Of course it was a jogger.
**** Mike
They always find them.
**** Sarah
Nobody else is up at six.
*** They duck under the tape. Sarah crouches. Studies something we cannot see.
**** Sarah
Where's forensics.
**** Mike
Twenty minutes out.
**** Sarah
Twenty minutes.
**** Mike
Traffic.
**** Sarah
At six in the morning.
**** Mike
It's the marathon today.
*** Sarah stands. Looks at the sky. Rain on her face.
**** Sarah
I should have stayed for the coffee.
*** Blackout                                                        :transition:
