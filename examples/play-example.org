* Introduction
*One Day* is a play that delves into the complexities of family dynamics, the passage of time, and the impact of unexpected events on relationships.

* CHARACTERS
|----------+---------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Bob      | An ordinary man who drives an ordinary van and roams the land.      |
| Cáit     | His neighbour who goes into labour then things get much stranger    |
| Mairéad  | Bob's sister she's brilliant at twister but it gives her a blister. |
|----------+---------------------------------------------------------------------|

* Synopsis
A play about the passage of time and the small moments that define us. Set in a suburban kitchen over the course of a single morning.

* Setting
A kitchen in a semi-detached house in Galway. The room is warm and cluttered. A window looks out onto a small garden. Morning light.

* Scene List
- Act I, Scene 1: Morning Tea
- Act I, Scene 2: The Revelation

* Act I
